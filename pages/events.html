Rebel Rally Cork

November 5th, 2024, 09:00 AM

Held in the heart of Cork, the Rebel Rally showcases classic, vintage, and modified cars from all over Ireland, celebrating Cork's "rebel county" spirit with exciting races, live music, and local food vendors.

location_on Address: Monahan Road, Ballintemple, Cork, T12 XY89, Ireland
phone Contact: 087-376-2378
Event Schedule
  • 10:00 AM - Rally Car Parade: Start the day with a parade featuring a lineup of top rally cars, showcasing both classic and modern models.
  • 12:00 PM - Coastal Rally Stage: Watch drivers tackle a timed rally stage along Cork's coastal roads, navigating sharp turns and rugged terrain.
  • 2:00 PM - Pit Stop Challenge: See teams compete to achieve the fastest tire change and refueling time, simulating real rally pit stops.
  • 4:00 PM - Spectator Ride-Alongs: Get up close to the action with limited spectator ride-alongs, where fans can experience the thrill of rally driving firsthand with a professional driver.

phone Contact: 087-912-1931
